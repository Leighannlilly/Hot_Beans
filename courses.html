W3 Schools:

W3 Schools is a well-known online platform that provides users with web development tutorials and references for different web technologies. This website offers a wide range of educational materials that are beginner friendly for those who might want to start web designing. It’s a very interactive site with examples on hands-on activities that helps one gain experience in web development.

Udemy:

Udemy is an online teaching and learning site with over 213, 000 courses and 62 million students. It was first founded in May 2012 by Eren Bali, Green Biyani and oktay Caglar. This site helps Students from all around the globe access education such as programming, marketing and data science.
